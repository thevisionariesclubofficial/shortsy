Shortsy ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process rental payments and maintain transaction records.
  • To personalise content recommendations.
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, OTP, password reset).
  • To improve the Service through analytics.
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / India DPDP Act)

  • Contract: Processing necessary to fulfil your rental and account contract.
  • Legitimate Interests: Security, fraud prevention, analytics.
  • Consent: Marketing communications (opt-in only).

4. Sharing of Data

We share data only with:

  • AWS (Cognito, Lambda, S3): Account management and content delivery.
  • Razorpay: Payment processing.
  • Firebase: Analytics and crash reporting.
  • Google (Sign-In): Authentication only.
  • Legal authorities: When required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain account data for the duration of your account's existence plus 3 years thereafter for legal compliance. Payment records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law. Usage logs are retained for 12 months.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate data via your profile.
  • Deletion: Request account deletion and data erasure.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise your rights, email privacy@shortsy.in.

7. Cookies & Tracking

The Shortsy app does not use cookies. Our web-based services use essential cookies only. See our Cookie Policy for details.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, bcrypt password hashing, and AWS IAM access controls. No system is 100% secure; please use a strong, unique password.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via the app or email. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
