Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 1, 2026

At Shortsy, we want you to be satisfied with every rental. This policy explains when refunds are granted and how to request one.

Quick Summary: Rentals are generally non-refundable once streaming begins. Refunds are granted for technical failures that prevent access.

1. General Policy

All rentals on Shortsy are digital goods delivered immediately upon purchase. Because access is granted instantly, all sales are final by default.

2. Eligible Refund Scenarios

We will issue a full refund in the following cases:

  • Technical failure: The content was completely inaccessible due to a platform error and you did not successfully view any portion of the film.
  • Duplicate charge: You were charged more than once for the same rental due to a payment processing error.
  • Unauthorised transaction: The charge was made without your authorisation (subject to investigation).

3. Non-Eligible Scenarios

  • You started watching the content and simply did not enjoy it.
  • The rental period expired before you watched it.
  • Slow internet connection on your end caused buffering.
  • You purchased the wrong title by mistake (we can offer a one-time courtesy credit).
  • The request is made more than 7 days after the transaction date.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email support@shortsy.in within 7 days of your transaction with:

  • Your registered email address.
  • The title you rented and the approximate date/time of purchase.
  • A description of the issue experienced.
  • Any screenshots or error messages (if applicable).

5. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days and credited back to the original payment method. Bank processing times may vary.

6. Creator Impact

We value our creators. Refunds for technical errors are absorbed by Shortsy and do not affect creator payouts.

7. Contact

Questions? Reach us at support@shortsy.in.
